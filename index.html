  One book One College Launch   August 31,2021
OBOC Event

Part one
 The launch of the One Book, One College program began on Tuesday. They had a Sustainability Fair, a plant-based food sampling, and a discussion about the book "A Life on Our Planet" by David Attenborough. We learned about some of the school's various clubs such as, The Book Club and The Sustainability Club. I was intrigued by the Opossum that the CROW (Clinic for the Rehabition of Wildlife) representative had. I have never saw one in person before nor so close to me. I learned from the ECHO organization that I can purify water by taking some Moringa Seeds by crushing it with a mortar, stirring it into the water, and letting it sit for a few hours. The impurities will settle to the bottom, and the top will contain the clean water. Additionally, they have a farm tour containing Ackee, a poisonous plant if it is not handled correctly, but it is my favorite food. Click on the underlined words to see a YouTube video on water purfication as well as pictures of ackee.

 I attended the discussion aspect of the day by going to a presentation by Micheal Sauer. I learned that the voice I always listened to voice was indeed David Attenborough.
